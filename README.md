## What is this?

**AI WARS** is an auto-simulation that visualizes the entire history of artificial intelligence — from Turing's 1950 paper to the Agent era of 2026.

This is not just a game. It's a **living documentary**.

We built this to look back at the AI war that has reshaped our world: the breakthroughs, the rivalries, the billion-dollar bets, and the people behind them. CEOs clash, papers explode onto the scene, nations enter the fray with their flags held high — and through it all, the timeline of human history scrolls beneath, reminding us where we've been.

**Why does this exist?**

- To **witness** the AI war that's been fought across decades, companies, and continents
- To **remember** the pivotal moments — AlphaGo's Move 37, ChatGPT's 100M users in 5 days, the Transformer paper that changed everything
- To **reflect** on where humanity goes from here — when agents write code, models reason at olympiad level, and the line between human and machine intelligence blurs

The simulation runs itself. You just watch, learn, and think about the path forward.

## Features

**Timeline Simulation (1950 — 2026)**
- Auto-playing timeline from Turing Test to Agent Era
- 17+ milestone events with paper/model classification (LLM, Vision, GenAI, Agent)
- Historical images flash on screen as breakthroughs occur

**30 AI CEOs & Leaders**
- Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Jensen Huang, Dario Amodei, and more
- Each appears with their portrait and a historic quote
- Mini avatars persist on the battlefield, reacting emotionally to events
- Deceased leaders (Steve Jobs) quietly fade away

**Global AI Nations**
- Companies from USA, South Korea, China, France, Japan, UAE, India, Germany
- National flag banners announce each country's entry into the AI war
- NAVER, Mistral, DeepSeek, Sakana AI, and more

**Battles & Rivalries**
- Deep Blue vs Kasparov, AlphaGo vs Lee Sedol, GPT-4 vs Claude vs Gemini
- Collision dialogues between CEOs and AI models
- Combo system with escalating visual effects

**Human History Ticker**
- Bottom timeline shows major world events alongside AI history
- From WWII to COVID-19, contextualizing AI within the human story
- Current year highlighted as the simulation progresses

**Company Intelligence**
- Click any company in the power rankings to see their model history
- Tagged by type: LLM, Vision, GenAI, Agent, Paper, Hardware
- Real-time power rankings updated as events unfold

**Shareable Results**
- Final rankings with Top 10 companies
- Share to X (Twitter) with AI quotes from Turing, Hawking, Musk, and more
- Every simulation uses a random seed — each run tells a slightly different story

## Contributing

**Missing a historical event? Wrong year? Important figure not included?** We want this to be as complete and accurate as possible.

If you know of an important AI milestone, paper, company, or moment that should be included — or if you spot an incorrect date or missing detail:

1. [Open an issue](https://github.com/leaf-kit/aiwars.game/issues) with the event details
2. Include: year, what happened, why it matters, and any relevant links
3. We'll review and add it to the next deployment

**You can actively participate in improving the timeline** — fixing wrong years, adding missing events, suggesting new characters or models. All contributions are welcome — whether it's a forgotten paper from the 1980s, a breaking event from last week, or a correction to an existing entry.
